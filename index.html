President Boakai Condemns Recent Israel and Iran Conflict, Calls for Restoration of Peace.

President Joseph Nyuma Boakai, Sr., has condemned the recent escalation of violent conflict between Israel and Iran and finds it deeply regretful that political and diplomatic efforts have yet to bring peace and stability to the Middle East.
