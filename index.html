President Boakai Condemns
Recent Isreal and Iran
Conflict,Calls for Restoration
of Peace.

President Joseph Nyuma Boakai,Sr.,has
condemned the recent escalation of violent
conflict between Isreal and Iran and finds it deeply
regretful that political and diplomatic efforts have
yet to bring peace and stability to the Middle East.
